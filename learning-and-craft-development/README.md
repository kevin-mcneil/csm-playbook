# Learning and Craft Development

Books that support this playbook:

- Customer Success: How Innovative Companies Are Reducing Churn and Growing Recurring Revenue  
  Frameworks and principles for building a customer-outcome-focused CS practice, grounded in examples from leading    SaaS organizations.

- Storytelling With Data  
  How to turn messy data into clean, compelling visuals and narratives for Quarterly Business Reviews and executive presentations.

- Unreasonable Hospitality  
  Mindset for creating memorable, personalized, high trust customer experiences and reducing friction.

- Digital Customer Success: Why the Next Frontier of CS Is Digital and How You Can Leverage It to Drive Durable Growth
  Explores digital-first customer success, automation, scaled programs, and how modern CS teams blend human touch with digital engagement to drive consistent, durable growth

Suggested habits:

- Weekly product deep dive session
- Weekly industry or article read, especially in your customers domain
- Regular review of your own emails and decks to refine clarity and storytelling
