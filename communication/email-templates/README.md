# Email Templates

This folder contains all email templates provided in the playbook. Templates created through the AI Companion System prompts are stored here exactly as written.

## Introduction Email for a New Stakeholder

Generated from the AI Companion System prompt (Communication Template Prompt):

Using the stakeholder table we created, generate four communication templates including an introduction email for a new stakeholder explaining our partnership vision.

Template structure:

- Open with a specific pain point or opportunity relevant to their role
- Acknowledge their current approach and its limitations
- Explain how the solution delivers unique value
- Include a clear next step
- Keep it under one hundred fifty words and easily scannable

## At Risk Account Message

Generated from the AI Companion System prompt (Communication Template Prompt):

An at risk account message addressing potential adoption challenges.

Template structure:

- Empathetic acknowledgment of the challenge
- Clear restatement of the customer goal
- Direct description of the adoption challenge
- Offer a short, clear plan to correct it
- Keep the message straightforward and supportive

## Expansion Opportunity Message

An expansion opportunity message tied to their business objectives.
